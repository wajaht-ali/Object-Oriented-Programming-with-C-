Inheritance:-
    When a child or (derived) class inheritance the properties of its parent or (base) class then this is known as the concept of Inheritance.

Types of Inheritance:-
    - There are three types of inheritance.
     -> Single inheritance
        = Parent to child
     -> Multilevel inheritance
        = Parent to Parent to Child
     -> Multiple inheritance.
        = From two parents to one child.
    -> Hierarchial inheritance
        = Two childs inherits from one Parent
